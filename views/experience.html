    Pilkington Automotive Argentina

    Pilkington, the company where I started as a professional. I was studying Industrial Engineer and in that days, the only thing I wanted to do, was to work in a factory.

    That's the reason why I started to work as a Process Engineer trainee. Mi office was located in the middle of the factory, sourronded by productive processes. Pilkington is an automotive glass manufacturer company, all around the world.

    One year and a half as a trainee. I understood how "things" were manufactured. From its design up to the finished product. I learned operational excellence, 5S, looking up for improvements and measuring them with KPI's, some of them self-designed.

    After that, I started to work in the Quality department. This is where I currently work. As a Quality Engineer, I learned mainly two things, that nowadays are my pillars as a professional: Customer relationaship and troubleshooting.

    What I understood for working in Quality, is that the most important thing of the product we manufacture, is the customer experience. In Quality we have one responsability: Customer satisfaction and doing things right.

    From early 2021, I am in charge of the Quality department, working with a huge team: 5 engineers, 2 engineer trainees and 2 supervisors, which they have there own teams. In total, 46 people working in my team.

    Colegio Santa Inés

    I was a teacher of a math workshop for 10 years old girls. Here is where I discover one of the passions I have: Teaching.

    The best of teaching, is that I don't need to do exclusively that in my life. We can teach everywhere. In a professional field, in a familiar field, with friends, wherever we want.

    Don't ask me to leave teaching, because I won't do it at all. I will continue teaching, even though I have nothing to give. I will always find the way for doing it. Maybe reasearching or asking to someone else, because passion for teaching borns from another passion: Learning.

    Northstar California Resort
